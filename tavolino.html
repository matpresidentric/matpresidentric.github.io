Chromate Coffee Table

Coffee table rectangular designed by Luigi Caccia Dominioni . The table is designed for Palace of paper Binda of Milan.


Characteristics

Coffee tables rectangular in shape, with legs in chromed steel and steel frame painted in basalt, gray, blue, green, bordeaux and ocher or mat black. Plans in molded glass 15 mm thick.

Model - Details

The edge is where after resting above the glass parallelepiped is constructed with two cuboid to size placed one above the other to form the T perimeter. The legs are created with the method ROTATIONAL_SURFACE and placed above a disc of black color and another as a base of the leg created with the function CYL_SURFACE and inserted at the corners between the edges. Finally, it is simply assembled all.
